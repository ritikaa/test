Facility Management is an interdisciplinary field devoted to the coordination of space, infrastructure, people and organization. However, Facility Management facilitates on a much wider range of activities than just business services and these are referred to as non-core functions. Facilities Management is of significance to organizations of all kinds and, as an emerging discipline, it has become the focus for the important issues of best value and customer satisfaction within the management of supporting services. Well-managed services enable an organization to function at its most efficient and effective level, offering real added value improvements to the organization's core business.

Facility Management is thus a modular facilities management system that works together with your business and allows day-to-day management of the processes, automates operations, provides a management dashboard for critical analysis and enables enterprise wide control in the most intelligent and effective way. It integrates the principles of business administration, architecture and the behavioral and engineering sciences.
